About VSAS

A dependable system for a distributed workforce

Visual Vertex Staff Attendance System gives organizations a straightforward way to manage attendance without sacrificing accuracy or accountability. Built for teams that work across locations and time zones, VSAS helps HR and management maintain professional, reliable records — while giving staff a fast, frictionless way to check in every day.

This is the first phase of a complete Staff Management Platform, designed from day one to grow into authentication, QR-code attendance, dashboards, reports, and more.
